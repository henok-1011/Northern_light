A Few Words About Our Center

Northern Lights is based on the belief that children who participate in appropriate challenges and activities that require them to think critically, think outside the box, socialize, and challenge themselves and others will succeed in their daily activities such as education, social life, public speaking, and creativity in their future lives.

All of these factors are followed in Norwegian daycares, resulting in a great educational system in Norway. Adult literacy exceeds 99 percent, and school expectancy for a five-year-old in Norway was 17.7 years in 1998 and 17,9 in 2002, one of the highest school expectancy rates of any country.
